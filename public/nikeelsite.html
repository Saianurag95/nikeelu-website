My Story

Visionary Digital
Coach & Innovator

I believe technology should not be limited to big cities, English-speaking professionals, or people with technical backgrounds. It should reach every student, creator, entrepreneur, professional, homemaker, and rural dreamer in a language they understand and in a way they can use in real life.

My journey has always been about bridging the gap between rural potential and digital opportunity. As a digital coach, entrepreneur, speaker, and founder, I have worked on initiatives like Digital Villages, Startup Utsav, Digital Connect, Digipreneur.AI, and Telugu AI Bootcamp to help people learn practical digital skills, AI tools, automation, branding, freelancing, and business growth.

Through Telugu AI Bootcamp, I started making AI education simple, practical, and accessible in Telugu. What began as a training program has grown into a movement where students build portfolios, freelancers automate their work, creators grow their personal brands, and business owners use AI to generate leads and scale faster.

For me, this work is not just about teaching tools. It is about giving people confidence, clarity, and opportunity. My mission is to help 100,000+ Telugu people learn about AI in Telugu and use it practically for education, careers, creativity, business, and everyday growth.

My story is still being written through every learner who starts believing in their own potential, every business that grows with digital skills, and every community that gets access to the opportunities of the AI era.
